Background

CudaWare is an e-commerce website which had published
in May 2021. It is offering a desktop, laptop and accessories.
After they received some feedback from the consumers about
they should add a benchmark feature that allows users to
enter their PC parts and the system will automatically analyze
their setup performance (FPS) in the games, CudaWare updated
this feature on their website after a month of development.

Objective

The purpose of creating a Computer Building service is to help consumers to reduce money spent on a pre-built computer of a brand, where all the computer components are made with the same manufacturer. With CudaWare, customers have a wide variety of brand selections on the parts that they would like to buy and build a computer with the parts from different manufacturers. The website also offers a benchmark feature where users or customers can analyze their setup gaming performance before they make a purchase. Customers can also buy an individual component from the website such as RAM if they would like to upgrade their existing PC.

Intended Audience



CudaWare knows that their customers are in need of performance, speed while reducing the cost of building one. They target desktop, laptop, workstation, casual/home users, youths, and budget PC builders. They provide a wide variety of brand selections which means that it is affordable for consumers who are low on budget. CudaWare is aimed at customers who range from 12 to 40 years old, they can be either gamers, casual users, work from home entrepreneurs, or a student who is moving to secondary school. They target customers that are single and most who are at the high school level or higher that are dealing with studies or who are free most of the time with an annual household income of RM2,000 or below. They target customers that need speed yet want affordable products. Customers that play demanding games like PUBG, GTA V, Cyberpunk 2077, etc.
